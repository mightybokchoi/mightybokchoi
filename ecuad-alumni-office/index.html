1. The Frustration Landscape

Breakdown of primary complaints from I've been hearing. Click the list items to explore the qualitative feedback behind the numbers.

Solicitation Fatigue

"Every email feels like it's asking for money before offering any value."

Core Issues

  • Donation requests sent too frequently
  • Lack of transparency on fund allocation
  • No clear value proposition for donors

Career Support Gaps

"I graduated years ago and still haven't received any meaningful career resources."

Core Issues

  • No job board or industry connections
  • Mentorship programs are non-existent
  • Skills workshops not offered post-graduation

Communication Breakdown

"I don't know what's happening at ECU unless I actively search for it."

Core Issues

  • Newsletters feel generic and impersonal
  • Important updates buried in noise
  • No segmentation based on interests

Networking Void

"There's no easy way to connect with other alumni in my field."

Core Issues

  • No alumni directory or platform
  • Events are rare and poorly promoted
  • Regional chapters don't exist

UX/Process Friction

"The website is confusing and I can never find what I need."

Core Issues

  • Outdated and clunky website interface
  • Multiple logins required for different services
  • Forms are lengthy and redundant
